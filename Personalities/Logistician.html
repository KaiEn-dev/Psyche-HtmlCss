The Logistician.

Responsible and having tireless dedication to duty, these people tend to be reserved yet willful, with a rational outlook on life. They compose their actions carefully and carry them out with methodical purpose.


Advantages.

  • Honest and Direct
  • Strong-willed and Dutiful
  • Very Responsible
  • Calm and Practical
  • Create and Enforce Order
  • Jack-of-all-trades

Disadvantages.

  • Stubborn
  • Insensitive
  • Always by the Book Judgmental
  • Often Unreasonably Blame Themselves

Strategies to Study.


  • Study alone instead of in study groups, to maximize concentration and eliminate distractions.

  • You may benefit by making flashcards, timelines, or any method that makes knowledge more tangible.

  • Have "mock" debates to organize information into different lines of thinking.

  • Use a planner to set deadlines and plan ahead
