The Debater.

Quick witted, Debaters tend to be bold and creative, deconstructing and rebuilding ideas with great mental agility. They pursue their goals vigorously despite any resistance they might encounter.


Advantages.

  • Knowledgeable
  • Quick Thinkers
  • Original
  • Excellent Brainstormers
  • Charismatic
  • Energetic

Disadvantages.

  • Very Argumentative
  • Insensitive
  • Intolerant
  • Difficulty to Focus
  • Dislike Practical Matters
    (Where creativity is not required)

Strategies to Study.


  • Have study groups and discussions with fellow students.

  • Make mindmaps and brainstorm "what if" questions.

  • Have "mock" debates to organize information into different lines of thinking.

  • Write down the key deadlines, but you can adjust your daily schedule as new opportunity arises.
