The Logician.

Inventive and creative, these flexible thinkers enjoy taking an unconventional approach to many aspects of life. They often seek out unlikely paths, mixing willingness to experiment with personal creativity.


Advantages.

  • Great Analysts and Abstract Thinkers
  • Imaginative and Original
  • Open-minded
  • Enthusiastic
  • Objective
  • Honest and Straightforward

Disadvantages.

  • Very Private & Withdrawn
  • Insensitive
  • Absent-minded
  • Condescending
  • Loathe Rules & Guidelines
  • Second-guess Themselves

Strategies to Study.


  • Study alone instead of in study groups, to maximize concentration and eliminate distractions.

  • Make mindmaps and brainstorm "what if" questions.

  • Have "mock" debates to organize information into different lines of thinking.

  • Write down the key deadlines, but you can adjust your daily schedule as new opportunity arises.
