The Protagonist.

Charismatic and passionate, these warm, forthright types love helping others. They tend to have strong ideas and values. They back their perspective with the creative energy to achieve their goals.


Advantages.

  • Tolerant
  • Reliable
  • Charismatic
  • Altruistic
  • Natural Leaders

Disadvantages.

  • Overly Idealistic
  • Too Selfless
  • Too Sensitive
  • Fluctuaring Slef-Esteem
  • Struggle to Make Tough Decisions

Strategies to Study.


  • Have study groups and discussions with fellow students.

  • Make mindmaps and brainstorm "what if" questions.

  • Read up on real-life applications of the subject at hand to help you relate and remember the materials.

  • Use a planner to set deadlines and plan ahead
