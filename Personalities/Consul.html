Traits.

- Extroverted

- Observant

- Feeling

- Judging

The Consul.

Consuls are attentive and people-focused, and they enjoy taking part in their social community, being social butterflies. Their achievements are guided by decisive values, and they willingly offer guidance to others.


Advantages.

  • Strong Practical Skills
  • Strong Sense of Duty
  • Very Royal
  • Sensitive and Warm
  • Good at Connecting with Others

Disadvantages.

  • Worried about Their Social Status
  • Inflexible
  • Reluctant to Innovate or Improvise
  • Vulnerable to Criticism
  • Often Too Needy
  • Too Selfless

Strategies to Study.


  • Have study groups and discussions with fellow students.

  • You may benefit by making flashcards, timelines, or any method that makes knowledge more tangible.

  • Read up on real-life applications of the subject at hand to help you relate and remember the materials.

  • Use a planner to set deadlines and plan ahead.
