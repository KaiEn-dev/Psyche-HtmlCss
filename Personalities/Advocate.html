The Advocate.

With a deep sense of idealism and integrity, Advocates tend to approach life with deep thoughtfulness and imagination. Their inner vision, personal values, and a quiet, principled version of humanism guide them in all things.


Advantages.

  • Creative
  • Insightful
  • Principled
  • Passionate
  • Altrustic
    (to behave in a way that
    will help the people around them)

Disadvantages.

  • Sensitive to Criticism
  • Reluctant to Open up
  • Perfectionistic
  • Avoiding the Ordinary
  • Prone to Burnout

Strategies to Study.


  • Study alone instead of in study groups, to maximize concentration and eliminate distractions

  • Make mindmaps and brainstorm "what if" questions.

  • Read up on real-life applications of the subject at hand to help you relate and remember the materials.

  • Use a planner to set deadlines and plan ahead
