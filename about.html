About Psyche.


What?

What is Psyche?

Psyche is a project done by a group of students from the University of Nottingham for the Software Engineering Group Project module.

Our Mission

We aim to provide students a platform where they can discover themselves, understand what makes them tick and why, and easy access to seek the counsel of professionals.

Why?

Why use Psyche?

Psyche gives you an opportunity to assess yourself and to understand the ways you'll be able to study efficiently and effectively. It is also an easy way to link yourself to counsellors, as the website will be able to send your test results to the appointed counsellor with consent.
